Our Founder Jennie Kim

Today, I stand before you to celebrate the remarkable journey of Jennie Kim, a true trailblazer not only in the world of music but now in the beauty industry as well. We all know her as the fierce, talented, and iconic member of BLACKPINK, but what many might not realize is that Jennie is also making waves as a visionary makeup founder.9 Her beauty is undeniable, but Jennie's influence goes far beyond her stunning appearance. She has always captivated us with her confidence, her unique sense of style, and her authenticity. As a global ambassador for renowned brands and a fashion icon, Jennie has had a front-row seat to the beauty industry's ever-evolving landscape. This experience, paired with her unrelenting passion for all things beauty, has led her to launch her own makeup brand—one that reflects her personal vision of beauty, empowerment, and inclusivity. Jennie’s makeup line is more than just a collection of products; it’s a movement. A movement that challenges the conventional standards of beauty and invites us to express ourselves boldly, authentically, and without limitations. Whether you're a makeup enthusiast or a newcomer, Jennie’s brand empowers every individual to explore and embrace their own beauty. With her diverse, innovative range of products, she's ensuring that no one is left out. The beauty world is finally seeing a brand that speaks to people of all backgrounds, encouraging everyone to feel beautiful in their own skin. What makes Jennie's line so special is that it’s not just about the products—it’s about the experience. The sleek packaging, the high-quality formulas, and the inclusivity of her collection are all reflections of Jennie herself. She has always been someone who knows exactly what she wants and isn't afraid to take risks, and her makeup line is the perfect extension of that mindset Jennie Kim’s journey from being an international pop star to becoming a beauty mogul is a testament to her ambition and drive. She has proven that true artistry knows no boundaries. By creating a makeup line that inspires and empowers, Jennie continues to shape not just music, but beauty, leaving an indelible mark on both industries. So here's to Jennie Kim—a visionary who is redefining beauty on her own terms. May her entrepreneurial spirit continue to inspire all of us to chase our dreams and embrace our unique qualities.
